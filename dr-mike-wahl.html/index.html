Education & Experience

Dr. Wahl's commitment to dental excellence began at Case Western Reserve University, where he earned his Bachelor of Arts in 1982, followed by his Doctor of Dental Surgery (DDS) degree in 1985.

He joined the practice alongside his father in 1986, carrying forward a family tradition of patient-centered care. Since 1996, he has served as the sole owner, guiding Wahl Family Dentistry with a steady hand and a commitment to modern, compassionate dentistry.

Research & Publications

Beyond his clinical practice, Dr. Wahl is a dedicated scholar. He is a published author, contributing extensively to the dental field through books and numerous articles in respected medical journals, reflecting his dedication to advancing dental science and evidence-based practice.

Beyond the Clinic

When not providing exceptional care to his patients or contributing to academic research, Dr. Wahl enjoys a fulfilling life outside the office. He is an avid racquetball player, maintaining an active lifestyle. A passionate student of American history, he enjoys delving into the past. Above all, he cherishes quality time spent with his family.
